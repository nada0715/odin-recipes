Sanji's Humba

Best served during dinner with lots of rice :)

Ingredients

  • one large onion
  • garlic
  • Pork belly
  • Pineapple juice
  • Salted black beans
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Banana blossoms
  • Dried bay leaves
  • Peppercorn
  • Sugar

How to Cook Humba

  1. Dice one large onion and mince 5 cloves of garlic
  2. Toss in your pork belly and let it cook until it browns
  3. Add in the onion and garlic slices you have and cook, until your onions soften
  4. Then, add in your 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, peppercorn, and bay leaves
  5. Next, pour in 1 cup of pineapple juice and bring your humba to a boil
  6. Cover the pan and let your dish simmer until your pork becomes tender
  7. Wait 30 to 40 minutes
  8. Add in 2 tablespoons of vinegar and wait for your mixture to re-boil
  9. Let your dish simmer for about 3 minutes, then spoon in the salted black beans
  10. Add in your brown sugar as well, then give your humba 5 minutes more
  11. Lastly, take your dried banana blossoms and toss them into the pot
  12. Wait 5 to 8 minutes and bwala its ready to served

Serve this dish with copious amounts of rice; its bound to be a hit! Share and enjoy it with the rest of your family today!
